Jun Han

Han Hyeong-jun (한형준), known by his stage name Jun Han (준한), is a South Korean singer-songwriter and musician under JYP Entertainment. He is the lead guitarist of the South Korean idol rock band Xdinary Heroes.

  • Birth Date August 18 2002 (age 22)
  • Current Roommate: None
  • Nicknames: Rockstar Rabbit/Bunny; "Junaedi"

  1. Education
  2. Career

Education

Jun Han attended Gimhae Hwalcheon Elementary School, Gimhae Gaya Elementary School, and Hwalcheon Middle School.

Career

2021: Debut with Xdinary Heroes

On November 1, 2021, a teaser with the slogan "Heroes Are Coming" was released, causing speculation that JYP Entertainment would be introducing a new group shortly thereafter. This new group was later revealed to be Xdinary Heroes. On November 18, Jun Han was revealed as the fourth member of Xdinary Heroes. The group made their debut on December 6 with the digital single "Happy Death Day".
